What telehealth suits

What we can help with

Telehealth is well suited to many everyday healthcare needs. If your doctor decides you're better seen in person, they'll let you know and help arrange a visit.

  • Repeat prescriptions and medication reviews
  • Referrals to specialists and allied health
  • Test and pathology result discussions
  • Mental health support and care plans
  • Follow-ups and management of ongoing conditions
  • Medical certificates and general advice

Care that fits around your day.

Good to know before you book

Telehealth appointments are billed in line with our standard fee schedule and are available to patients who have seen a doctor face to face within the last 12 months. Please check your phone number when booking and be reachable from 5 minutes before your appointment; consult times can run late, so stay available for up to 30 minutes after your scheduled time. Your doctor will call twice; if you can't be reached, the appointment will need to be rescheduled. Medicare does not permit telehealth consultations while you are overseas. In a medical emergency, please call 000.
