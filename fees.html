Fees & billing

Clear, upfront information about our fees, bulk billing and what to expect at your appointment.

Home / Fees & Billing

Billing Policy Update · Effective 1 July 2026

To keep providing high-quality, sustainable care, our billing policy is changing from 1 July 2026. Concession cardholders and children 15 & under continue to be bulk billed on weekdays. The out-of-pocket fees below apply to all other patients.

AppointmentWhoOut-of-pocket

The full consultation fee, including the Medicare rebate, is charged at your appointment, with the rebate processed back to your account afterwards. Fees may vary with the length of consultation. The out-of-pocket amount stays the same for valid Medicare cardholders. Telehealth appointments are billed in line with this schedule.

By consultation type

Consultation fees

Out-of-pocket amounts by consultation type and day. Skin checks are not bulk billed.

Consultation typeWeekdaySaturdaySunday

Standard, long and prolonged consults may apply to skin checks, ear syringing and acupuncture, so the full fee varies but the out-of-pocket cost stays the same.

Procedures

Procedure fees

Out-of-pocket amounts for procedures performed on-site. Select any procedure on Find Care to see full fees, the doctors who perform it and what to expect.

Good to know

What to expect

Bring your Medicare card

Please bring your Medicare card and any concession or DVA cards so we can process your rebate correctly.

Payment on the day

The full fee is settled at your appointment, with the Medicare rebate returned to your account shortly after.

Let us know if plans change

Please give notice if you can't make your appointment, so the time can be offered to someone else and to avoid the no-show fee.
