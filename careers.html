Our culture

Small team, first names, long relationships

Balwyn Central is the kind of practice where everyone knows each other, and where our doctors know their patients by name because they've cared for the same families year after year. Continuity here isn't a slogan; it's just how the place works.

Our GP directors are practising doctors in these rooms, not distant owners. They consult in the same corridors, share the same tea room, and make decisions as people who understand exactly what the work feels like. When something needs fixing, you talk to a colleague, not a head office.

It's a supportive, non-hierarchical environment. People look out for each other, cover for each other, and genuinely enjoy each other's company. If that sounds like the sort of medicine you've been wanting to get back to, we'd love to meet you.

The people who make this place what it is.

About the clinic

A proper general practice, done properly

We care for families across Balwyn and the eastern suburbs: everyday general practice alongside skin cancer medicine, women's health, chronic disease care, minor procedures, health assessments and antenatal shared care. Registered nurses are on-site whenever we're open, and pharmacy and pathology sit in the same building.

You'd be joining a team of experienced GPs and registrars across two comfortable Whitehorse Road locations, with private consulting rooms and well-equipped treatment rooms. Our second site at 411 is separately accredited in its own right, which says something about how we like to run things: properly, and without shortcuts.
