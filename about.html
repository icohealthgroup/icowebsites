Who we are

Our medical practice

Established in 2015, Balwyn Central Medical is a fully-accredited general practice and part of ICO Health Group, one of Victoria's growing primary healthcare providers. We operate as a modern, family-oriented centre offering a wide range of health services under one roof.

Across our two Whitehorse Road sites you'll find comfortable waiting areas, private consultation and treatment rooms, on-site pharmacy and pathology, and full-time nursing support, so more of your care can be handled in the one place, on the one visit.

  • Fully-accredited general practice
  • Bulk billing available for eligible patients
  • Telehealth, after-hours and weekend access

How we operate

Simple, coordinated care

All appointments can be made through our online booking system or with our friendly reception team. New to the practice? We can arrange for your medical records to be transferred from your previous doctor so your history comes with you.

We warmly welcome new patients, and offer telehealth for the times coming in isn't practical. For anything urgent outside opening hours, after-hours support is available.
